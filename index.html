Pest Guide

Know your threat

Understanding pests helps you spot problems early. Click any pest to learn how it threatens your home and how we eliminate it.

🐜 Termites High Risk
Why it's dangerous

Termites are notorious for destroying timber construction through their cellulose-digesting ability. They can penetrate roofing, walls, and flooring silently — damage goes unnoticed until it's extensive. A building can require expensive structural repairs and complete renovation.

PCIS Solution

Pre-construction soil treatment (10 years warranty) and post-construction treatment (6 years warranty). We use the safest chemicals available to create a long-term protective barrier, designed to protect your entire structure.

🪳 Cockroaches High Risk
Why it's dangerous

Cockroaches spread food poisoning, salmonella, and dysentery. They crawl into cupboards, pantries, and food areas. A single female can lay around 240 eggs in a lifetime — ignoring the problem leads to rapid, exponential infestation.

PCIS Solution

Targeted gel bait and residual spray treatment eliminating cockroaches at source — including eggs. Effective for kitchens, restaurants, hotels, and homes. Safe for food preparation environments.

🐀 Rats & Mice High Risk
Why it's dangerous

Rats carry plague and dozens of diseases. A female can produce several hundred offspring per year. They gnaw through wiring (causing fire hazards), walls, and insulation. Most damage is structural and expensive to repair.

PCIS Solution

Comprehensive rodent management using bait stations, trapping, and exclusion methods. We address entry points to prevent re-infestation, protecting your property and family from disease and damage.

🦟 Mosquitoes High Risk
Why it's dangerous

Mosquitoes transmit malaria, dengue fever, and chikungunya — all serious diseases prevalent in Goa. They breed rapidly in stagnant water and can infest an entire locality quickly, posing a serious public health threat.

PCIS Solution

Larvicidal treatment targeting breeding sites, combined with adulticidal fogging to eliminate existing populations. We also advise on preventive measures to reduce future breeding on your property.

🛏️ Bed Bugs Medium Risk
Why it's dangerous

Bed bugs are wingless, oval insects 4-5mm long. They're blood-sucking parasites that disrupt sleep and cause intense itching. They spread via travel and infested furniture, and are notoriously difficult to eliminate without professional treatment.

PCIS Solution

Thorough heat and chemical treatment targeting all life stages of bed bugs. We treat mattresses, furniture seams, and walls. Our method ensures complete eradication so your family sleeps peacefully again.

🪲 Wood Borers Medium Risk
Why it's dangerous

If you see white powder in your wooden cupboards that returns after cleaning, you have wood borers. Powder post beetles reduce precious wood to dust silently — living and feeding entirely inside the wood. Infestations can remain hidden for years.

PCIS Solution

Injective treatment into bore holes combined with surface spray to kill larvae and prevent re-infestation. We identify all affected wood and provide a comprehensive treatment plan to save your furniture and fixtures.
